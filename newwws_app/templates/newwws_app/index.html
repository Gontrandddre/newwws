About NEWWWS

An original idea, convinced users.

  • 2019-2020

    First step: Idea

    After much feedback about the archaic methods of searching for news in editorial departments, we had the idea of creating an application that would simplify this search through a web tool. We came up with the idea of creating an application to simplify this search through a web interface.

  • 2021

    New tool is Born

    It is after more than a year of reflection and work that our NEWWWS solution was born. This solution will revolutionise the search for news wherever it comes from. NEWWWS will be accessible everywhere to everyone.

  • 2021-2022

    Next step

    The next step? Making international news data intelligible and visible. This data analysis will allow us to visualise general news trends and improve our solution with new features.
